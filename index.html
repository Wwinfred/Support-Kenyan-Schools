✨ About Us

🌱 A small dream, making a big difference

In the heart of Kenya, thousands of children dream of learning, growing, and creating a better tomorrow. But many schools still lack the basics — clean water, safe dormitories, computers, books, and even classrooms.

We believe every child deserves a fair start.
That's why Support Kenyan Schools exists — to connect kind hearts with schools in need.

🤝 What We Do

We listen to schools. We list their needs. Then, we invite you — changemakers, supporters, and dreamers — to be part of the story.

💡 Why It Matters

When a school has the right tools, students thrive. Girls stay in school. Boys believe in their future. Communities grow stronger.

🎒 Join the Movement

This isn’t just our story — it’s yours too. Be part of a movement that’s changing classrooms, one brick, one book, one smile at a time.
